About Common Source of Precision

Our Mission

To be the definitive, curated repository for primary sources and reference data, providing uninterpreted and authoritative information to researchers, students, journalists, and the curious public.

Our Principles

Accuracy First

Every source is verified for authenticity and directly linked to its origin. We maintain the highest standards of data integrity.

No Interpretation

We present sources in their original form, without editorial commentary, analysis, or political bias.

Universal Access

Knowledge should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background, affiliation, or geographical location.

Source Transparency

Clear attribution, metadata, and provenance information for every document and dataset in our collection.

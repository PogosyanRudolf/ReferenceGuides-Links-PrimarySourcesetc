About Common Source of Precision

Our Mission

To be the definitive, curated repository for primary sources and reference data, providing uninterpreted and authoritative information to researchers, students, journalists, and the curious public.

Our Principles

Accuracy First

Every source is verified for authenticity and directly linked to its origin.

No Interpretation

We present sources in their original form, without editorial commentary or analysis.

Universal Access

Knowledge should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background or affiliation.

Source Transparency

Clear attribution and metadata for every document and dataset.

Why Primary Sources Matter

In an age of information overload and misinformation, the ability to access and verify original sources is more critical than ever. Primary sources provide the foundation for:

  • Academic research and citation
  • Evidence-based journalism
  • Legal and policy analysis
  • Historical understanding
  • Informed public discourse

Common Source of Precision — Curating the foundation of knowledge.

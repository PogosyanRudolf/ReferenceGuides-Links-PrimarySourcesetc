Frequently Asked Questions

How can I suggest a resource?

Use the contact form above and select "Resource Suggestion" as the subject. Include the resource URL and a brief description.

Do you offer translation services?

We're always looking for volunteer translators to help expand our multilingual content. Contact us if you'd like to contribute.

Can I use your resources for my institution?

Yes! All our curated resources are freely available for educational purposes. We encourage sharing and academic use.

How often is the content updated?

We regularly review and update our resource database to ensure all links are working and content remains relevant.
